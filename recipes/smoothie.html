Smoothie

Description

Be it Breakfast, lunch, or Dinner, there is no right time for a yummy Peanut Butter and Banana smoothie. This is an easy to follow and quick smoothie recipe.

Ingredients

  • 2 bananas, broken into chunks
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1/2 cup peanut butter
  • 2 tablespoons honey, or to taste
  • 2 cups ice cubes

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Place bananas, milk, peanut butter, honey, and ice cubes in a blender.
  3. Blend until smooth, about 30 seconds.
  4. Enjoy!
