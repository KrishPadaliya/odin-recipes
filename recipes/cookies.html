Description

  • The perfect lasagna recipe with creamy tomato sauce and perfect layers.

    • Ingredients

    • Meat
    • Onion and garlic
    • Tomato
    • Sugar
    • Spices and seasonings
    • Lasagna noodles
    • Cheeses
    • Egg

    Steps

    1. Make the meat sauce.
    2. Cook the noodles.
    3. Make the ricotta mixture.
    4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe intrsuctions.
    5. Cover with foil and bake.
    6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
